Our story

StayBeyond was born from the simple idea of bringing families together in beautiful handpicked homes across the UK.

We want our guests to enjoy special moments in our bespoke holiday rentals without compromising on the creature comforts that come with a hotel stay...

Family time needs a family home

We wanted to create an experience the whole family can be part of: the freedom to have breakfast together in your pyjamas, cosy nights spent in front of the fire, and drinks in the garden while the children play on the lawn.

In just a few years, StayBeyond has grown from managing just a handful of properties in London, to a fast growing business transforming how people travel.
